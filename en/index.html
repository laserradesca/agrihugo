Cascina La Cà

La Serradesca Società Agricola

Qualcosa

Cascina La Cà is a place for curious people to enjoy authentic farm produce and experience first-hand how it is grown, harvested and prepared. Our workshops, courses and events are run in both English and Italian languages, and focus on the local environment in a global context, encouraging a deeper understanding of how our world works, and inspiring the courage to think differently and act more thoughtfully.

FIND YOURSELF AMONGST THE HILLS AND FIELDS

The farmhouse is situated on just over eight hectares of agricultural land in the Serradesca valley, Bergamo, with half of the area dedicated to the cultivation of fruit, berries, hazelnuts, wine grapes and aromatic plants, as well as a honey bee apiary. The rest of the land is preserved as wild forest and meadow, supporting local biodiversity.
