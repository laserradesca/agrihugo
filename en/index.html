ITS NOT JUST ABOUT THE FOOD

Cascina La Cà is a place for curious people to enjoy local farm produce along with the stunning views in Bergamo’s Serradesca valley. You will see first-hand how everything is grown, harvested and prepared. Our workshops, courses and events are run in both English and Italian languages: enjoy a unique experience that will stick with you, and return home with a deeper understanding of our natural surroundings, and of where our food comes from.
